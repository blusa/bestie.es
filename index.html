With our Deep Learning Engine, Bestie selects the best pictures and lets you choose the ones you want.

Our models where trained to detect features from really good looking pictures so when you turn on Bestie's burst mode, all your pictures go through our engine and will be ordered by how well they look. In real time. Thanks to this, you will be able to take selfies and groupies with the main camera. Even with it's flash enabled.

Select the Emotions recognizer and even get better results.

You sure like better those groupies where everybody is happily smiling, or maybe you want to catch that picture where you surprised your children. With Bestie's emotions recognizer enabled, Bestie dinamically classifies all your images by the emotions people express. Somebody with their eyes closed? No problem. Bestie will help you ignore those pictures. We have it all covered.

To finish just select the picture, Bestie will select the best filter for it. Automagically.

We know you sometimes don't know whether your picture looks better with or without filter... but Bestie does. It will automatically select the best-looking filter for you or not filter at all. Still, if you're not sure and want to defy Bestie, you can of course scroll through it's wide selection of filters by your own as well. When you're done, You're just one click away from sharing it to the major social networks.
